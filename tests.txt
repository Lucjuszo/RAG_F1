	4. Why did Niki Lauda retire from the 1976 Japanese Grand Prix?
Niki Lauda retired from the 1976 Japanese Grand Prix due to his eyes being watering excessively due to his fire-damaged tear ducts and inability to blink. This made it unsafe for him to continue under the torrential rain conditions, which caused his car to retire after two laps.
